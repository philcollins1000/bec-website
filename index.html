What we get up to

BEC has been organising events for the Baldons villages for over a decade. Our flagship event is the annual Fireworks & Bonfire Night each November — drawing 800 to 1,000 people to the village green and providing the income that makes everything else possible.

That surplus subsidises Fun on the Green, the Ball, the Barn Dance and the Feast, provides the Christmas tree on the green and gifts for the village children, and allows us to make donations to organisations throughout the community.

See all our events →

Where the money goes

Over the years BEC has donated to the village churches, the youth club, hand bells, the Baldon Players, village halls, the Seven Stars, the Lunch Club — and most recently, a new computer for the village school.

We also invest in equipment — marquees, tables and everything needed to put on a great village event — which is stored at the village hall and available to the whole community.

See what we've funded →

Can you help?

Our events only happen because villagers give up their time to help. Whether it's putting up marquees, helping with the bar, marshalling car parking, or taking everything down at the end of the night — we always need willing hands.
