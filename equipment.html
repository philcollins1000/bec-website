Equipment hire

BEC owns marquees, tables and event equipment available to villagers and community groups at preferential rates.

Over the years BEC has invested in a range of equipment that we use to stage our own events — and which we make available to people in the villages at preferential rates. Whether you're hosting a garden party, a village gathering or a community fundraiser, we may be able to help.

All equipment is stored at the village hall premises. Japh Slaney looks after all of BEC's marquees — maintaining, repairing and booking them out. Contact Japh to check availability.
