Get involved

Volunteer, suggest an event, request a donation, or enquire about equipment hire. We'd love to hear from you.

Volunteer

Our events only happen because villagers give up their time to help. We always need people for:

  • Marquee erection — usually the Friday afternoon before an event
  • Marquee take-down — the morning after
  • Bar team — during events
  • Car parking — particularly for Fireworks Night
  • General helping hands — all the little things that make events work

No special skills needed — just willingness. Get in touch and we'll let you know when we need people.

Suggest an event or cause

Have an idea for a new village event? Know of a worthy local cause that deserves support? We hold regular committee meetings and welcome suggestions from anyone in the villages.

Equipment hire

To enquire about hiring our marquees, tables or other equipment, please contact Japh Slaney — he manages all of BEC's marquees, from maintenance and repairs to bookings. Details on the right.
